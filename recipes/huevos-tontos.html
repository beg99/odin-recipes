Silly eggs

In spanish is huevos tontos, but in english i prefer to say Dumbass eggs

Silly eggs recipe is Foolproof, so if you have two neurons or less. This recipe it's for sure your best

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • 2 eggs
  • Milk
  • Oil tuna
  • Garlic
  • salt
  • Pepper
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Slice the bread in little squares and put it in a bowl
  2. Pour some milk until the liquid is dry
  3. Zest an chopp the Garlic, Pour some tuna and then put it into the bowl
  4. Beat yout mea... Sorry, beat the eggs and then add it to the mixed in the bowl
  5. Whith a spoon make some kind of balls whith the Ingredients
  6. Fry your balls, until they get golden and crunchy
  7. Dry the excess oil with paper, and then ENJOY!
