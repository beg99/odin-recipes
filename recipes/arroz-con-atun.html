Arroz con atun

Yeah i know you want rice with tuna so stay tuned haha!

Rice with tuna this baby it's easy-make, if you think that your dumbass could never cook something you are definitly wrong. And the best TASTE LIKE GOD

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Tuna
  • Salt
  • Optional
    • Onion
    • Chickpeas
    • Mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Boil water NOW.
  2. When water is boiling, add rice. How many? It's your business.
  3. When rice is ready maybe in five or six minutes, dry the water and open a can of tuna.
  4. Mix the tuna with the rice and add salt as you please.
  5. LET'S FUCKING GO.
